Who We Are

Al Fanar Auto
Spare Parts

Established in 2002, Al Fanar Auto Spare Parts Trading has grown to become a trusted name in the UAE and GCC auto parts industry. Based in Industrial Area 2, Sharjah, we specialize in quality aftermarket spare parts for commercial vehicles including trucks, buses, and heavy equipment.

Our mission is to provide the right parts at the right time, with uncompromising quality standards and customer service that sets us apart in the competitive Middle East market.
